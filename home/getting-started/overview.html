Star Kingdoms is a free-to-play, space-themed, text-based, 4X strategy MMORPG, where you rule over a kingdom and compete for dominance with your friends in a universe filled with other kingdoms.

It will take not only skill, but teamwork, political acumen and sometimes sacrifice to navigate your path to victory.

The game itself operates in real time as a series of turns, called ticks, which process logic every 30 minutes at XX:00:00 and XX:30:00 to explore new lands, construct buildings, train military units and much more.

The primary server (S1) is reset for a new round every month and sign-ups are open for a few days at the beginning of each month. There is also a classic server (S2) which is based on a simpler, early version of the game. Our focus will be to familiarise you with the full version of the game on S1.

To get started, let's make an account and sign up here: https://starkingdoms.net/SignUp.aspx

Your kingdom name will be visible to everyone, and your ruler name will be revealed by reconnaissance and in-game messages. There is a limit of 24 characters.

If you are brand new to Star Kingdoms, the planet types Volcanic Inferno, Terra Form or Multiple Terrain offer a gentle introduction, depending which planet types are in rotation. Generally, the races Terran and Gistrami will start strong and allow you to set a good pace. If the round hasn't yet begun, select default, you can change your planet type and race later on.
