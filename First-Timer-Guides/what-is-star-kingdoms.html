What is Star Kingdoms?

Star Kingdoms is a free space strategy game where you make friends and enemies in a battle for universal domination.

The game website can be located here: https://starkingdoms.net/

Join our discord community server: https://discord.gg/nVKhtVw

How to use this Wiki

This Wiki is designed to be read both sequentially and as a reference guide. We will begin with the basic concepts of the game so you can get up and running as quickly as possible. For the seasoned player we will then cover the inner workings of the game mechanics and give you the insights to lead your team into battle!
